About Us


Bonda Luxury Cars epitomizes the pinnacle of automotive opulence, seamlessly blending cutting-edge technology with unparalleled craftsmanship to redefine the essence of luxury on four wheels. Renowned for its commitment to excellence, Bonda crafts vehicles that transcend the ordinary, embodying a fusion of elegance, performance, and innovation.

Each model is a testament to meticulous attention to detail, where precision engineering meets the artistry of automotive design. Bonda's fleet showcases an exclusive collection of vehicles that cater to the discerning taste of those who demand nothing but the extraordinary.

From the sleek lines of the exterior to the sumptuous interiors adorned with bespoke materials, Bonda Luxury Cars epitomizes the epitome of automotive sophistication, offering a driving experience that transcends the boundaries of mere transportation, elevating it to a realm of unparalleled luxury and refinement.

Lamborghini

The Lamborghini Aventador SVJ (Super Veloce Jota) is a high-performance variant of the Aventador. It combines cutting-edge technology with extraordinary design.
The SVJ features a redesigned V12 engine, active aero, and carbon fiber parts. It's known for its impressive aerodynamics and an improved power-to-weight ratio of 1.98 kg/hp.

F12 Berlinetta

Ferrari

The Ferrari F12 Berlinetta is a front-engine, rear-wheel-drive grand tourer produced by Italian automobile manufacturer Ferrari. It debuted at the 2012 Geneva Motor Show.
The F12 Berlinetta uses a naturally aspirated 6.3 litre Ferrari V12 engine, generating a power output of 740 PS (544 kW; 730 hp) at 8,250 rpm.
It was the most powerful Ferrari road car when it was launched. The car is known for its impressive aerodynamics and was replaced by the 812 Superfast in early 2017.

911 Turbo S

Porsche

The Porsche 911 Turbo S is a high-performance sports car. It's powered by a 3.75-liter boxer engine with two VTG turbochargers, delivering 478 kW (650 PS), which is 70 PS more than its predecessor.

DBS Superleggera

Aston Martin

The Aston Martin DBS Superleggera is a grand touring car produced by British manufacturer Aston Martin since 2018. It's powered by a 5.2-litre twin-turbocharged V12 engine, generating a power output of 725 PS (533 kW; 715 hp).

720S

McLaren

The McLaren 720S is a sports car designed and manufactured by British automobile manufacturer McLaren Automotive.
It's powered by a 4.0 L M840T twin-turbocharged V8 engine, generating a power output of 720 PS (530 kW; 710 hp). F40

Ferrari

The Ferrari F40 is a mid-engine, rear-wheel drive sports car produced by Ferrari from 1987 to 1992. It was engineered by Nicola Materazzi with styling by Pininfarina. The F40 was powered by a 2.9 L twin-turbocharged V8 engine, generating a power output of 478 PS (352 kW; 471 hp).
It was designed to celebrate Ferrari's 40th anniversary and was the last Ferrari automobile personally approved by Enzo Ferrari. At the time of its launch, it was Ferrari's fastest, most powerful, and most expensive car for sale.
